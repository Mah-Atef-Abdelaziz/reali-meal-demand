---

# Project Overview

The company is a food service provider responsible for preparing meals for employees.

The company suffers from significant food waste because it prepares hot meals before knowing the exact number of employees who will actually request them.

Cold meals are not a problem because they can be stored, but hot meals cannot.

The goal is to use Artificial Intelligence to predict tomorrow's meal demand and reduce food waste while ensuring enough meals are prepared.

The system should also include an intelligent NLP chatbot that allows managers to interact with the system using natural language.

---

# Business Objective

Build an AI Decision Support System that can:

* Predict tomorrow's hot meal demand.
* Estimate food waste before it happens.
* Help kitchen managers decide how many meals to prepare.
* Reduce operational costs.
* Improve food planning.
* Generate AI insights.
* Answer business questions through a chatbot.

This is **not just a chatbot**.

The chatbot is only one module of the overall AI platform.
